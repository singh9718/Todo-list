What is Todo?

A Todo app is a software tool for task management. It helps users create, organize, and track tasks with features like due dates, priorities, and reminders. Users can collaborate and access tasks across devices, aiding productivity and organization. Popular examples include Todoist and Trello, ideal for managing to-do lists effectively.
